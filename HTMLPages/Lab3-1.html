Timeline of Basketball

1891

Dr James Naismith invents basketball.

 1896

The First known Professional basketball game is played in Trenton, New jersey.

 1946

The Basketball Association of America (BAA) is founded Maurice Podoloff is named leagues president.

 1947

The Philadelphia Warriors beat the Chicago Stags 4 games to 1, to win the first BAA championship.

 1949

Six team from the NBL join the BAA. The resulting 17 teams form the National Basketball Association (NBA). And name Maurice Podloff as President.

 1950

The Minneapolis Lakers become the first team to win back-to-back championshipns.

 1961

The Chicago Packers enter the NBA as the first expansion team.

 1966

The NBA awards Chicago with its third team, (the Previous two had either moved, or disbanded) the Bulls.

 1967

The NBA gives team to San Diego (ROckets), and Seattle (Supersonics).

 1979

The NBA Adopts a three-point line.

 1980

The NBA Admits the Dallas Mavericks bringing the NBA to 23 teams.

 1996

The NBA Agrees to start a Women's Nation Basketball Association (WNBA)

 2008

The Seattle Supersonics relocate to Oklagoma City and become the Oklahoma City Thunder, but as part of their agreement with the city for leaving they cannot keep the Sonics history and thus are usually considered an expansion team.
